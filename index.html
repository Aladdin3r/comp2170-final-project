How to Use the To-Do List

1. Enter a task in the input field.

2. Click the "ADD" button or press "Enter" to add the task.

3. Click on the checkbox to mark the task as completed.

4. Click the "Edit" button to modify the task.

5. Click the "Remove" button to delete the task.
